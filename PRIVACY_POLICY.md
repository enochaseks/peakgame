# Peak Card Game - Privacy Policy

## Overview
Peak Card Game is a free, multiplayer card game developed for your entertainment. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection
- **Local Storage**: Game progress and settings are stored locally on your device
- **Firebase**: Player data, game sessions, and scores are stored in our secure Firebase database
- **No Personal Information**: We do not collect names, emails, or personal identifiable information
- **Device ID**: Firebase automatically assigns a unique device ID for multiplayer functionality

## How Your Data is Used
- To enable multiplayer gameplay and real-time game sessions
- To persist your game progress between sessions
- To maintain game leaderboards (scores only)
- To improve game performance and stability

## Third-Party Services
- **Firebase (Google)**: Provides database and real-time multiplayer functionality
- **Analytics**: We may use anonymous analytics to improve the game

## Data Deletion
- You can clear all game data through the Settings menu
- Choosing "Clear Cache" will remove all stored data from your device
- To request deletion of server data, contact us

## Security
- Your data is transmitted securely over HTTPS
- Firebase enforces security rules to protect your information
- We do not share your data with third parties

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Continued use of the game constitutes acceptance of any changes.

## Contact
For questions about this privacy policy, visit our GitHub: https://github.com/enochaseks/peakgame
